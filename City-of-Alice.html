Meter Reader/Tech Duties

  • Read smart meters, old gear meters and auto-read meters
  • Drive around routers to pick up smart Mueller meters
  • Install lock boxes and smart meters
  • Turn on and off customers water if they are new, leaving or did not pay their water bill. (Most of the time we would lock the water valve)
  • Get rereads if needed for the Water Department
  • Call SetFlow or Mueller to get some of the smart meters reprogrammed or update software or firmware
  • Before clocking out we would take out the mail and make sure the flags are put up correctly
  • Help out with my Department with some tech issues if needed
  • Was in the safty committee
  • Was able to get my Meter Reader department e-mails, work phones, desks, and work computers
  • Troubleshoot my departments devices if needed
  • Worked over time if needed
  • Download and Upload routers for billing
  • Helped rewrite some of the dress code rules for HRs handbook
  • Keep file work of work orders and meter change outs
  • Made sure trucks were drivable and safe
  • Dropped of money to the bank or collect money from the bank if needed for the Water Department
  • Active SetFlow EndPoints and call it in to make sure they are active and on the GPS on SetFlow website
